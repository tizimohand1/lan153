Module 9

AI-Powered Meta Prompts & Interfaces

Explore AI platforms, interfaces, and build custom meta-prompt tools

🛠️ 7 Resources

🎯 Learning Objective

Evaluate leading AI tools and large language models (LLMs) — including MS Copilot, ChatGPT, and Claude — for classroom applicability and pedagogical fit.

💡 Why This Matters

The AI tool landscape shifts every few months. Educators who can confidently compare, evaluate, and demonstrate the leading AI platforms have an immediate advantage — in the classroom, in curriculum conversations, and when institutions need informed voices at the decision-making table.

👥 How This Fits Your Role

📚 Instructors

Discover which tools match your discipline and teaching style. Walk into class ready to show students what these platforms can actually do — not just describe them.

🔬 Professors

Compare platforms for research support, writing assistance, and scalable student feedback. Build peer-credible, evidence-based opinions on each tool's capabilities and limits.

🏛️ Administrators

Evaluate tools for institutional adoption, licensing considerations, and alignment with your AI acceptable use policies — before committing to a platform.
