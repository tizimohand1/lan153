Module 6

Build AI-Powered Instructional Materials

From lesson plan to chatbot — build it all

📚 21 Lessons

🎯 Learning Objective

Create AI-generated instructional materials tailored to diverse learner needs, including lesson plans, assessments, study guides, and multimedia resources.

💡 Why This Matters

The real payoff of AI literacy is what you can build with it. This module moves from theory to creation — giving you hands-on experience generating the actual course materials you'll use in your classroom, fully customized to your discipline, your students, and your teaching style. You leave with a portfolio of real deliverables, not just concepts.

👥 How This Fits Your Role

📚Instructors

Build a complete set of AI-generated classroom materials — lesson plans, assessments, flashcards, and more — all tailored to your own course content and ready to use immediately.

🔬Professors

Design sophisticated instructional sequences and discipline-specific assessments, then deploy an AI-powered course chatbot that supports your students outside of class hours.

🏛️Administrators

Understand what AI can produce at institutional scale — from accessibility-enhanced course materials to chatbots that extend faculty reach and improve student support equity.
