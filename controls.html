Security Assessment Scenario

As the newly hired cybersecurity analyst for FreshFlow Systems, you've been tasked with completing a comprehensive security controls assessment. The company has experienced rapid growth (125% annually) and operates critical OT systems for food packaging. Recent concerns include potential MFA gaps, OT network segmentation issues, and the need to maintain <2-hour recovery time for production systems. Your assessment will help prioritize security investments and ensure compliance with food safety regulations while supporting continued business growth.

Technical Security Controls Assessment

Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
Implementation of MFA across all cloud applications and critical systems to prevent unauthorized access through compromised credentials.
High Risk

High Risk

High Risk

Medium Risk

Medium Risk
Automated Backup & Recovery

High Risk

Comprehensive Video Surveillance
