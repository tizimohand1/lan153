Module 8

Instructional Graphics & Learning Activities

Visual learning and interactive engagement across disciplines

📚 3 Lessons

🎯 Learning Objective

Design and deploy AI-powered tools and interactive applications for your specific academic discipline — experiencing firsthand how AI can generate immersive, classroom-ready learning experiences across any field.

💡 Why This Matters

The most powerful proof of AI's value in education is seeing it applied in your own field. This module moves beyond general AI tools and into discipline-specific applications — interactive simulations, data dashboards, case study generators, and subject-matter chatbots. Each tool was built by educators, for educators, and demonstrates exactly what AI-enhanced instruction looks like in healthcare, business, HVAC, literature, cybersecurity, and more.

👥 Who Benefits

🔬STEM Instructors

Explore biology simulations, HVAC diagnostics, and cybersecurity career tools that show how AI can make complex technical content interactive and self-paced.

📊Business & Econ Faculty

See AI-generated economics simulations — from money supply flows to labor demand curves — and dashboards that bring data to life for students who learn by doing.

📚Humanities & Social Science

Experience interactive literary analysis tools and case study wizards that transform passive reading into active inquiry — applicable across any text-based discipline.
